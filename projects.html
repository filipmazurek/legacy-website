Improved Imaging Transforms

I am currently working with the Nightingale Lab at Duke University. I am working to optimize image transforms, ideally to real time. Currently, image data is collected in radial slices, meaning that it is in a spherical coordinate system. Then, the data is transformed into the Cartesian coordinate system. This is a computationally expensive operation, which I aim to speed up for convenient clinical use.
