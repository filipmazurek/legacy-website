High-Speed Photography

This is an ongoing semester project for my digital photography class. In it, I am interested in capturing the moments right as some momentuous change occurs. For example, I started with water droplets hitting a water surface to caputre the moment of the droplet hitting the surface of the water, bouncing off of it, and pulling up a column of water along with it.
